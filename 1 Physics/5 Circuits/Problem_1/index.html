Theoretical Background

The Lorentz force equation:

$$\mathbf{F} = q (\mathbf{E} + \mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{B})$$

For a uniform magnetic field (and optional electric field):

$$\omega_c = \frac{q B}{m}, \quad r_L = \frac{m v_\perp}{q B}$$
